🖥️ How to get the stream URL (PC)
1
Open the streaming site on Brave/Chrome on your PC with adblocker active
2
Press F12 → go to Network tab → filter by Media or search .m3u8
3
Press Play on the stream — you'll see a .m3u8 request appear in the Network tab
4
Right-click it → Copy URL → paste it here
5
If the URL expires next session, repeat. Many live channels have stable URLs that last a long time.
